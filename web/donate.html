Support the project

DataPurge is free, open-source, and volunteer-maintained. Donations help us keep the lights on and the brokers annoyed.

What donations cover

Hosting

Cloudflare Pages and Workers keep the site fast and free to use. Domain registration and DNS management.

Email delivery

The drip email service uses Resend to deliver opt-out requests reliably on behalf of users who sign up.

Development time

Building features, fixing bugs, keeping templates current with new privacy laws, and maintaining the codebase.

Broker research

Tracking 700+ data brokers requires ongoing research — verifying opt-out methods, adding new brokers, and updating broken links.
