Why use the app?

📱

Works offline

The full broker registry and legal templates are bundled in the app. No internet needed to generate opt-out emails.

🔔

Deadline notifications

Get reminded when a broker's legal response deadline passes. Never miss a follow-up window.

🔄

Auto-updated broker list

The app checks for new brokers and updated opt-out methods from our GitHub registry. You control when to update.

🔒

Your data stays on your device

Just like the web version, your personal information never leaves your phone. Everything is stored locally.

How it works

1

Install and enter your info

Name, email, and location. Stored on your device only.

2

Tap to send opt-outs

Each broker opens a pre-filled email in your email app. Hit send and move to the next.

3

Get notified

The app reminds you when legal deadlines pass so you can send noncompliance notices.

🔒

Privacy-first, even on mobile

The app contains no analytics, no trackers, and no ads. Your personal information is stored locally on your device. The only network request the app makes is to check for updated broker data from our public GitHub repository — and only when you choose to update.

Prefer the browser?

DataPurge works great in any web browser too. No installation needed.
